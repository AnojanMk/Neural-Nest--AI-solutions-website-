## 📋 Overview

**NeuralNest** is a modern, responsive AI-focused website designed to showcase artificial intelligence services, solutions, and company information. Built with a sleek futuristic aesthetic featuring glassmorphism effects, dark theme, and smooth animations.

### 🎯 Purpose

- Professional presentation of AI services
- Build brand credibility in the AI industry
- Generate leads through contact forms
- Showcase company expertise and team
- Educate visitors about AI solutions

---

## ✨ Features

### 🎨 Design Features

- Glassmorphism effects with blur backgrounds
- Dark theme with vibrant neon accents
- Beautiful gradient elements throughout
- Smooth scroll animations and micro-interactions
- Fully responsive on all devices

### 🚀 Functional Features

- Neural network particle animation in hero section
- 3D flip service cards with back details
- Animated number counters for statistics
- Contact form with validation and notifications
- FAQ accordion with expandable sections
- Team profiles with social media links
- Back to top button with smooth scrolling
- Loading spinner animation

### 📱 Sections

1. **Hero** - Animated gradient text with CTA buttons
2. **Services** - AI consulting, ML, Neural Networks
3. **Solutions** - Analytics, Automation, Cloud AI
4. **About** - Company info and key features
5. **Team** - Expert profiles with social links
6. **Testimonials** - Client reviews carousel
7. **Blog** - Latest articles and insights
8. **FAQ** - Frequently asked questions
9. **Contact** - Contact form with validation
10. **Footer** - Social links and newsletter signup
